Available Classes

Insanity is a cardio class, based on max interval training. This class will push you past your limits with athletic/plyometric drills mixed with intervals of strength, power, resistance & core training. No equipment or weights needed. You don't have to be in shape - varying levels of each exercise are provided.

Saturday mornings beginning January 7th, from 7:15 - 8:00 am

Class Dates: January 7, 14, 21 & 28, February 4 & 11.

PiYo is a hybrid, athletic workout, which combines the mind/body practices of yoga and Pilates, as well as the principles of stretch, strength training, conditioning and dynamic movement. The great music and moves make for a fun and intense, yet low-impact workout. You’ll sweat, stretch, and strengthen – all in one PiYo workout! Students may bring a yoga mat, towel, and water.

Monday evenings beginning January 2nd from 6:15 - 7:00 pm

Class Dates: January 2, 9, 16, 23 & 30, February 6

Country Heat LIVE is the jump-right-in-and-do-it dance workout that is so much fun you'll barely notice you're working up a sweat and getting a total-body workout. It is a high-energy, low-impact, and easy-to-follow dance workout perfect for all fitness levels. Students may bring a bottle of water, towel, and smooth soled tennis shoes with minimal grip.

Tuesday evenings beginning January 3rd from 6:00 - 6:45 pm

Class Dates: January 3, 10, 17, 24 & 31, February 7

Yoga

Yoga for Athletes

Gain the edge over your competition by building more powerful muscles, flexibility & balance. Adding yoga to your training regimen can also reduce the risk for injury. Be prepared to sweat and learn how to be mentally tough in uncomfortable situations. Recommended gear - yoga mat, yoga block & yoga strap.
